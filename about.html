About Me

I’m a poet, speaker, and storyteller based in the Pacific Northwest. My work explores themes of memory, longing, resistance, and the blurred space between tenderness and grief.

✍ Creative Practice

My writing blends confessional lyricism with historical memory. I draw inspiration from city streets, ancestral wounds, protest songs, and late-night conversations. Each poem begins with a question I don’t yet have an answer to.

🎤 Performance & Publications

I’ve performed at TEDxUTAustin and served as Houston’s first Youth Poet Laureate. My work appears in The New York Times, among others.

🔧 Current Projects

I’m currently developing my first full-length poetry manuscript and designing a guided audio tour that preserves erased Black histories in Austin, Texas.

🌲 Fun Facts

When I’m not writing, I’m exploring old neighborhoods, listening to jazz, or attempting to bake a firm banana cream pie.
